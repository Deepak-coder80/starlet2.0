About Us

Mind Empowered Charitable Trust (ME) is an NGO that creates mental and physical health awareness and provides skills for the personality development of youth and women (18-25 years).

What ME do ?
  • Conduct weekly webinars, Q&A sessions.
  • Mental and Physical health awareness sessions in colleges.

Learn More

Women Empowered (WE) is an initiative by Mind Empowered Charitable Trust aiming

  • To empower women (18-25 years).
  • To provide the girls with a safe space and an open forum where they can ask their queries related to their body anonymously from a genuine expert than use Google to get answers.
  • To improve physical wellbeing and female safety by simple techniques of self defense.
What WE do ?
  • Conduct Technical sessions in various domains like cybersecurity, web development, machine learning.
  • Organize technical events like Ideathons and Hackathons.
  • Meetups with women entrepreneurs to inspire young girls.
  • Gynaecological sessions with an expert Gynaecologist.
  • Self-defence workshops in collaboration with Vanitha Police.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Should I be in a team when submitting my idea?

    We encourage students to participate in teams of 2-4 students. If you don’t have a team, apply anyways and we will help match you to a team here.

  • Can I participate remotely?

    You can participate in STARLET's hybrid hackathon in both online and offline modes. We invite you to engage in offline mode.

  • Which programming language should I know in order to participate?

    Any programming language is fine, in order to participate

  • Should I bring food?

    No! There will be arrangements for food and snacks for all for FREE!

  • Who can participate in the STARLET hackthon?

    Anyone (between age 17 and 25) who identifies as a woman or a member of a minority gender.

  • Does the event cost anything?

    No. It is completely free.

  • I am a beginner, can I participate?

    Absolutely. We will have mentors and resource available. Come learn and experience your first hackathon at STARLET.

  • Is the team leader the only one who wants to register? 

    No. Registration is individual. You can form your team on the day of the hackathon.
